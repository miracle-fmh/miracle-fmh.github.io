Dongdong Yu

I am a Researcher in the Visual Computing Team at ByteDance AILab. My research interests focus on the computer vision and artificical intelligence, specifically on the topic of image classification, human keypoint, and scene parsing. I obtained PhD from CASIA in 2017 supervised by Prof. Ruwei Dai and Prof. Jie Tian.

Email: yudongdongatcasia@gmail.com; yudongdong@bytedance.com
